Who are we?

Mathieu Duponchelle

Mathieu Duponchelle, Pitivi developer since 2010 is the one who started, the port to the GStreamer Editing Services during the Google summer of code (GSoC) 2011. This work was achieved under Thibault Saunier mentoring. He has been since then very involved in the Pitivi project and is now a core member of the Pitivi community.

In 2013, he worked again as a GSoC student, this time in order to bring the port of Pitivi to GES to a releasable and usable state. Thanks to his hard work and his close collaboration with the GStreamer community, he fixed a ton of bugs in all the components of Pitivi and became a very active member of the GStreamer project as a whole.

Thibault Saunier

Thibault Saunier joined the PiTiVi project as a Summer of Code student in 2010 and has been hooked ever since. He has been the main developer of the GStreamer Editing Services since 2011, and has been implementing all the feature that were needed by Pitivi but were not yet implemented at the time of the porting. He has been investing a massive amount of his free time making sure that Pitivi was going in the right direction and making sure the GStreamer Editing Services were the solid basis Pitivi needs to fully leverage the power of GStreamer.

Thibault as been working for collabora since 2011 on GStreamer itself, and thus has pretty deep knowledge of the inside of the framework. He is one of the 5 main commiters on GStreamer during the last 12 moth.

Our engagment

We are very passionate about the project, and this is why we are working on our free time. We worked on Pitivi and all its surrounding components for years now, and we believe that our track record should prove us competent to successfully complete the goals we are setting here.

During the project, we plan to make sure to inform the community of our progress, first on a daily basis on the #pitivi IRC channel on freenode, and then through regular blog posts (at least one every two weeks).
